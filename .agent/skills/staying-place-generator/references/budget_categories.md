# Reference: Budget Category Definitions

## Price Level Mapping
Use the following mapping for the Google Maps MCP `price_level` (1–4) attribute:

- **Budget (Price Level 1)**: Basic accommodations, hostels, or simple guesthouses. Prioritize safety and central location over amenities. [2]
- **Mid-range (Price Level 2)**: Standard 3-star hotels or highly-rated boutiques. This is the default "balanced" choice. [2]
- **Upscale (Price Level 3)**: 4-star hotels with full amenities (gym, room service, concierge). [2]
- **Luxury (Price Level 4)**: 5-star properties, luxury resorts, or high-end flagship hotels. [2]

## Selection Logic
When generating the "top 5 amicable places," the agent must:
1. Include at least one result from three different price levels to provide user variety.
2. Cross-reference ratings (min 4.0) with these budget tiers to ensure quality. [2]
